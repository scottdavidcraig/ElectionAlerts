Terms & Conditions

This section explains the terms which govern your use of our service out sets out how we may use the information you send us.

Key Points

The Election Alerts service is open to residents of the UK who are eligible and registered to vote.

Your information will be used for the purpose of providing you with election alerts.

You will never receice any party politcal messages from Election Alerts
