What are election alerts?

What is an Election Alert?

An Election Alert is a notficiation that an election is talking place. Sign up and we will send you a message to let you know when an election is happening.

Why should I sign up?

Many people either don't know or dont remember about all the elections in which they are entitled to vote. By signing up you can make sure you never miss a chance to cast your vote.

Why are you doing this?

We think everyone should have the opportunity to exercise their right to vote. But we know that life can sometimes get in the way.

Will it work?

We think so. Some super smart scientists think so too. Have a look about what they have to say.

Do you need some help?

Yes!

Our aim is to help 1 million people to vote. If you think you can help us to do that then we'd love to hear from you. Contact us here.
